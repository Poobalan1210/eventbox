## Animation Timings

- **Activity Transition**: 300ms ease-in-out
- **Entrance Animations**: 500ms with spring physics
- **Button Hover**: 200ms
- **Results Bar Growth**: 500ms ease-out
- **Winner Reveal**: Staggered 100ms per winner
- **Waiting Pulse**: 2s infinite loop
